Irish Setter

The Irish Setter is a high-spirited gundog
known for grace, swiftness, and a flashy red coat.

  • Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Height: 27 inches (male), 25 inches (female)
  • Weight: 70 pounds (male), 60 pounds (female)
